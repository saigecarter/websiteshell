📝About my project:

I am still fleshing out ideas for my project, but one of the assignments I completed during my quarter abroad was a paper called, "Taiko Across Time" for a class I took called Music of Japan.

The use of Taiko drums have so many different uses across different types of musical ensembles that have developed over time, from Gagaku (imperial court music) to ensembles that consist soley of different kinds of Taiko drumbs.

Additionally, I explored the spread of Taiko geographically, how iconic Taiko dojos were established in the 60's and 70's during the Asian American Movement, and how it has played such an important role in Japanese and other Asian American diaspora communities across the world. I think for my project, I would like to use a timeline or map visualization, or a combination of both.
